SequoiaView is a really cool disk space analyzer. I guess its been around a while but I had never heard of it. Also works on Windows7/R2 x64.

http://www.win.tue.nl/cgi-bin/usr/sequoia/download3.cgi

Then there is another one a friend just told me about called WinDirStat which is a very slick (And free). I like the breakdown by drive/folder and by file type. Also works on Windows7/Server 08 R2 x64.

http://windirstat.info/
